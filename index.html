Visual Media

The layers of craft involved in making a piece of visual narrative always astonishes me. Celebrated Polish director Krzysztof Kieślowski once famously said in an interviw. To pharaphrase it: "The USSR authorities would involve at every point of my film making process. They would tell me to edit some parts in the story. I would oblige and do the required edits and then they would ask me to chop off some parts in the screeplay and I again would agree to it and do the needful. It doesn't end there. They would axe some dialogues and even interevene in visual shot formations. In a way it worked well for me. I learnt to express in the confinity of censorship. It improved my craft. I have followed, involved in the visual media for long time.I also have produced, directed, wrote visual stories, songs. Combining the passion and skillset, I successfully established Content-Partnership networkings and projects"
